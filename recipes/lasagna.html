Lasagna

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. The dish is typically baked until golden and bubbly, creating a delicious combination of textures and flavors. It is often seasoned with herbs like basil and oregano, adding depth to its taste.

This hearty and comforting dish is enjoyed worldwide, with variations that include different types of cheese, meats, and even vegetarian alternatives. Whether served as a homemade meal or at a restaurant, lasagna remains a favorite comfort food, perfect for family gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce
    • 500g (1 lb) ground beef or ground turkey
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 800g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
    • 200g (7 oz) tomato sauce or passata
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Béchamel Sauce (Optional but Recommended)
    • 50g (3.5 tbsp) butter
    • 50g (⅓ cup) all-purpose flour
    • 500ml (2 cups) milk
    • Salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg
  • Other Ingredients
    • 12-15 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or no-boil)
    • 250g (9 oz) mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • 100g (3.5 oz) Parmesan cheese, grated
    • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Prepare the Sauce - In a pan, sauté chopped onions and garlic in olive oil. Add ground beef (or a vegetarian alternative) and cook until browned. Pour in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Let it simmer for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Pasta - Boil lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. (Skip this step if using no-boil noodles.)
  3. Make the Béchamel (Optional) - In a saucepan, melt butter, add flour, and stir for a minute. Gradually pour in milk, whisking continuously until the sauce thickens. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna - In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of sauce at the bottom. Add a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by sauce, béchamel (if using), and grated cheese (like mozzarella and Parmesan). Repeat the layers until the dish is full, finishing with a generous amount of cheese on top.
  5. Bake - Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and bake for 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  6. Serve and Enjoy - Cut into portions and enjoy your homemade lasagna with a side of salad or garlic bread!
